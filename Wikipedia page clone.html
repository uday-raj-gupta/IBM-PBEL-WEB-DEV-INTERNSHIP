Early life and education

Uday Raj Gupta is born in Bettiah West Champaran Bihar, India. He attended ( Primary Schooling ) from Albert Einstein School Bettiah Bihar then after ( Secondary Education ) Ramkrishna Vivekananda Vidya Mandir Bairiya West Champaran Bihar then after ( Senior Secondary Education ) Ramlakhan Singh Yadav College Bettiah West Champaran Bihar and then ( Graduation B.TECH ) from Vivekananda Global University Jaipur Rajasthan India.

During the college 2nd year he started his YouTube channel "YP LOGIX".

Career

YP LOGIX started his YouTube journey at a young age and gained popularity through tech-related content and college-related videos.
